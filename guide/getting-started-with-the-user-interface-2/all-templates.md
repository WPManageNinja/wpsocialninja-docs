# Templates

## Templates of WP Social Ninja 

As you have gone through the** Platforms**, now you can see in the given screenshot we have a** Templates** option.

![Templates](/guide/public/images/getting-started-with-the-user-interface-2/all-templates/WP-Social-Ninja-Platforms-4-1-5.png)

_Templates_

You can view the Templates list on the **Social Reviews **and **Social Feeds **in this section. To show this on other pages, you can use the** Shortcodes**.

![Templates | Shortcode](/guide/public/images/getting-started-with-the-user-interface-2/all-templates/WP-Social-Ninja-Templates-4-1-5.png)

_Templates | Shortcode_

However, you can change the design and layout in this **Template Segment**. For instance, you have made your Template before &amp; now you need to** Edit** your layout.

In that case, you don’t need to create a new Template for that. You can change it from the previous settings, click on **Edit,** or you may** Delete** or **Duplicate** it as well.

![Edit | Delete | Duplicate](/guide/public/images/getting-started-with-the-user-interface-2/all-templates/WP-Social-Ninja-Templates-5-1-5.png)

_Edit | Delete | Duplicate_

As you can see, you have three options available: **Edit, Delete** or **Duplicate** (see the above screenshot)

If you click on Edit, it will take you straight to the Template. Moreover, clicking on Duplicate will keep your original Template unchanged. You can copy this now &amp; change your Duplicate Template in any way!

![Amazon Template](/guide/public/images/getting-started-with-the-user-interface-2/all-templates/Amazon_Template-5.png)

_Template_

The best part is that the settings will not change and you can also resume your design at any point anytime.
